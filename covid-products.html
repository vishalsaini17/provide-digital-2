Covid-19
Response

We developed several bespoke applications to help ease the pressure on the medical structures during the pandemic. We created apps that facilitated demands for hospital beds, frontline medical workers and Covid-19 test results.

Bed Bureau

Img here

Find a Bed easy and quick

The Bed Bureau is a web based application that aims to help its users keep a track of the availability of beds in a hospital. The public dashboard of the app informs a user about the hospitals and the status of beds available. The user is updated with information about open beds and unavailable beds for 7 days.

Provide My Booking

Img here

Get a seat and stay safe

The Provide My Booking app was developed keeping in mind the need for social distancing and crowd control to stop the spread of Covid-19. The app is designed to help its users in booking seats for themselves, whether it is for a medical consultation or any other event. The app allows you to book yourself a seat and avoid over crowding.

Provide My Report

Img here

All your reports at one place

One stop shop for all your tests. The pandemic brought with it, a list of tests that our frontline workers needed to get done for their safety. The app helps consolidate all these tests results in one place. The app can also be used to booking vaccination appointments. All the details regarding the vaccine get saved up at one place.
