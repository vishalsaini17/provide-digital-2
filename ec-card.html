eC-Card

The eC-Card is a unique app for young people aged 16 to 24. It enables them to access condoms and sexual health services for free from sexual health service providers closest to them.

Collect Local Data

Sexual health services can compile statistics on condom distribution to help control supply and compare it to other data, such as teen pregnancies and STI outbreaks.

Highly Customisable

The app can be modified to suit the requirements of a health provider depending on the local area they cater to, with accurate geolocation technology. The eC-Card app has extensive customisation options.

Private User Registration

The users can register without using their identifying information such as their names, they can maintain anonymity and their privacy while looking for resources.

Why this app?

The eC-Card is a first of its kind smartphone app that enables sexual health service providers to offer free condoms to young people, making access to sexual health resources easier than ever before.
