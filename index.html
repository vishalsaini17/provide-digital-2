    PRODUCT

    eC-Card

    The accessible, secure and confidential condom card app offering free sexual health resources to young people.

    Collect Local Data

    Statistics on condom distribution can be collated by sexual health services to help control supply and compare with other data, such as teen pregnancies and STIs.

    Highly Customisable App

    Healthcare providers can modify the app according to their requirements. Customisation can be done based on the location and the needs of the customers.

    Private User Registration

    Service users can register without entering their don’t need to enter identifying information, such as their name in order to use the eC-Card app, their privacy is maintained.
